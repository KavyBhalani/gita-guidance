# Overcoming Jealousy and Envy: Gita Insights

Jealousy and envy are two of the most destructive emotions that can consume our lives. They can lead to feelings of resentment, anger, and even hatred towards others. But what if you could overcome these negative emotions and live a life of peace, love, and compassion? The Bhagavad Gita, an ancient Indian scripture, offers profound insights on how to overcome jealousy and envy.

## The Root Cause of Jealousy and Envy

According to the Bhagavad Gita, jealousy and envy arise from a sense of separation and disconnection from the divine. When we feel disconnected from our true nature, we become identified with our ego and begin to compare ourselves with others. This comparison leads to feelings of inadequacy, which in turn fuel jealousy and envy.
